t-Distributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding with Survivor contestants

Visualizing Survivor contestants using t-SNE. The embedding includes features such as how many confessionals a contestant had, how long they lasted in the game, which US state they live in, etc. If a castaway has appeared on multiple seasons, they will have a separate point for each appearance.

Hover over a point to learn about the contestant it represents. Change the drop-down menu to change the color scale (legends coming soon!). Brush over the graph to zoom in, and double-click to reset the axes.

Raw data from Dan Oehm.

[1] Actually, US Survivor just started allowing Canadians to participate two seasons ago, and the winners of the last two seasons have been Canadian.
